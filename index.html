Guidelines

General

  • Speed limit: 10mph
  • Tickets must be displayed on dashboard at all times
  • No re-entry once you leave
  • Use of photography or video is strickly prohibited

Parking

  • Enter and park at your own risk.
  • 2 cars between each set of poles.
  • If you prefer to sit outside, please be sure to sit in front of your car, to allow other customers to park.
  • No reserve spaces - parking spaces are first come, first serve.
  • Lift gates and rear doors cannot exceed the height of your vehicle. Twine to tie down is available at the refreshment center.

Movie Courtesy

  • Respect your neighbors. Refrain from loud talking during the movie. Absolutely no obscene language while on property.
  • Use your parking lights when entering or leaving the Holiday Auto Theater when the movie is running.

Safety

  • Do not leave your car running during the movie.
  • Before leaving, ensure the area around your car is clear of other movie guests.
  • Everyone must wear shoes when outside your car.
  • Children should be accompanied by an adult.
  • No alcohol on property at any time.

Other Information

  • The purchase of a $7 Food Permit is required when brining outside food into the theater.
  • Dog are allowed, but must remain on leash and quiet. All "presents" must be picked up and properly disposed of by the owner.
  • The play area closes when the movie starts.
  • No cooking, grilling, or preparing food of any kind in the theater.
  • Management reserves the right to refuse admission on tickets and to designate where cars may be parked. We assume no liability for accidents while on the premises.
